World of Olfactory

Collective passports were created to allow any citizen from the World of Olfactory to experience the fond and peculiar memories of other citizens.

A scent reminds us of a memory, and a memory transports us back into a place we previous experienced. Each individual can smell six varying familiar scents, and can write their associations to allows other citizens to transport themselves into their own different world for a moment.

Thus, each memory association serves as an individual passport stamp to a location.

Some scents include laundry detergent, coffee, and peanut butter ("it reminds me of going to get spring rolls with peanut sauce after hockey practice," "it reminds me of baking peanut butter hershey kiss cookies with my grandma for Christmas").
