Saccharine Journey

As a child, locations and places were remembered not through maps, but with senses like sound and sight. I experimented with conveying an unconventional journey of a daily route I take using the wayfinding methods I used as a child. By pressing each gummy bear (my favorite candy of choice), a picture on the way to my destination and sound associated with that location will trigger on the screen.
